Our Mission

Want to know what to do to get into college and when? Register with College Tracker and and plan out all
the steps it takes to get into the college that is right for you! Upon registration, the user will have access to college resources,
an interactive planner and various tools to aid in your search for additional financial assistance. Attending college doesn't have
to be a distant dream. Use College Tracker as your step-by-step guide onto the road to college and make it your reality.
